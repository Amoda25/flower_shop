Our History

Where Every Petal Has a Purpose

Bloomi Flowers was born in 2015 in the heart of Colombo, founded by Amara Perera — a passionate florist who believed that flowers are not just decorations, but vessels of emotion. The name "Bloomi" symbolises the natural beauty of flowers in full bloom.

What began as a small boutique shop on Flower Road quickly blossomed into one of Sri Lanka's most beloved floral brands. We built our reputation on three pillars: the freshest blooms, bespoke craftsmanship, and heartfelt customer service. Within two years, we expanded to offer island-wide delivery and custom wedding & event floristry.

Today, Bloomi serves thousands of customers each year — from individuals buying a single rose to couples planning their dream wedding. Every arrangement we create carries the same love and intention as that very first bouquet.
